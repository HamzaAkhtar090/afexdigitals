FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

How long does a typical project take?

MVPs typically take 6-10 weeks. Larger products run 3-6 months. We'll give you a detailed timeline during our free discovery session — no surprises.

What's your pricing model?

We offer fixed-price for well-scoped projects and time-and-materials for evolving ones. Every engagement starts with a free consultation and detailed proposal.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Absolutely. Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer ongoing maintenance plans with SLAs, monitoring, and on-call engineering.

Can you work with our existing team?

Yes — we regularly embed with in-house teams. We use your tools, join your standups, and integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows.

What industries do you serve?

We've built for fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, logistics, SaaS, and more. Our team adapts quickly to any domain and brings cross-industry best practices.

Who owns the code and IP?

You do. 100%. All code, designs, and intellectual property are transferred to you upon project completion. We use standard work-for-hire agreements.
